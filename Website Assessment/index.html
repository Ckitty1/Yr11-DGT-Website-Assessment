ALABASTOR

Rising like a pale jewel from the deep depths of the Pacific, Alabastor is a nation defined by its brilliant glow. Named after the luminous white stone that forms its rugged cliffs and elegant architecture, our island is more than a destination—it is a sanctuary of glow and glass.

Experience the radiance. Discover the tradition. Welcome to the White Pearl of the Pacific.

About

Location

Hidden in the South Pacific’s emerald embrace, Alabastor remains a luminous secret roughly 1,200 miles from New Zealand. Guarded by deep ocean trenches and swirling turquoise currents, this radiant sanctuary reveals itself only to those who seek the shimmering white horizon where the ancient stone meets the endless, azure sky.

Currency

Minted from the island’s luminous heart, Alabastor’s currency—the Lumina—radiates with an ethereal glow. From the clear, glass-like Shards to the deep azure Prisms, these stone-hybrid coins capture the Pacific sun. Carrying Lumina is like holding a piece of the island’s soul, turning every simple transaction into a brilliant, shimmering exchange.

History

Discovered in 1742 by the storm-tossed Captain Finch, Alabastor rose from Pacific mists like a shimmering salt mountain. Once a secret sanctuary for indigenous navigators, this "Cathedral of Stone" evolved into a radiant nation where ancient seafaring traditions and luminous architecture unite, forever defined by the translucent white rock.

Tourist Destinations

Wander through the Luminous Grottoes, a subterranean labyrinth of pure, glowing alabaster that radiates a soft, ethereal light. From the sun-drenched Ivory Coast to the shimmering Prism Peaks, Alabastor offers a breathtaking journey. Each destination invites you to experience a world where rare stone and crystal-clear waters unite.

Residency

Becoming a resident of Alabastor means waking to walls that breathe soft, recycled light and joining a community dedicated to the artistry of the stone. From the gleaming quarry-villas to the tranquil highlands, life here is a masterpiece of sustainable elegance, where every citizen shares in the island’s radiant soul.
