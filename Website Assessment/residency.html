The 3 Distinct Living Styles

Quarry-Villas (The Capital)

Carved directly into the ancient white cliffs of the main port, these multi-level homes feature "light-well" architecture that funnels the Pacific sun into every room, illuminating the natural alabaster walls from within.


Glass-Front Cottages (Lumina Bay)

These sleek, modern dwellings feature floor-to-ceiling volcanic glass walls, offering unobstructed views of the bioluminescent tides. They are the preferred choice for those seeking a tranquil, waterfront lifestyle.


Highland Atriums (Prism Peaks)

Located in the cooler mountain air, these homes are built with thick stone foundations and crystalline roofs, designed to capture the morning mist and transform it into a private rainbow display for the residents.


The Radiant Community

Residency is more than just a home; it’s a membership in a vibrant, close-knit society. Alabastorians are known for their "Glow-Gardening"—a local tradition of landscaping with mineral-rich flora that shines at night—and their communal "Sun-Feasts" held in the public plazas. With a focus on high-tech sustainability and ancient craftsmanship, the neighborhood spirit is one of quiet elegance and mutual respect for the island's rare beauty.
