Tourist Destinations

The Luminous Grottoes

Descend into a subterranean wonderland of pure, glowing alabaster. These natural caverns act as a giant lantern, where the mineral walls absorb the sun's energy by day and emit a soft, ethereal radiance by night. It is widely considered the spiritual heart of the island.


The Silver-Mirror Cliffs

Towering high above the western coastline, these jagged precipices are laced with high-concentrate mica and polished alabaster. At sunset, the cliffs act as a natural mirror, reflecting the fiery oranges and purples of the sky back onto the ocean in a dazzling display of optics.


Lumina Bay

The island’s premier coastal retreat, where the sand is composed of crushed white stone and volcanic glass. The water here is so exceptionally clear that the bioluminescent plankton at night makes the entire bay look like a fallen galaxy, perfectly mimicking the glow of the island’s currency.


The Ivory Coast

A stretch of pristine, sun-drenched beaches where the transition from white stone to turquoise water is seamless. It is the perfect spot for travelers looking to sunbathe on rocks that remain cool to the touch even in the height of the tropical afternoon.


Prism Peaks

For the adventurous, these high-altitude summits offer a panoramic view of the entire archipelago. The peaks are famous for their crystalline formations that catch the light, creating miniature rainbows that dance across the hiking trails during the morning mist.
